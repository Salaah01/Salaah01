VisualCSV

VisualCSV is an online platform where CSVs can very quickly and easily be converted into graphs.

How Does it Work

With every CSV you upload store the data as well in a database. We also ensure that the system is aware that the data you have uploaded belongs to you only.

Once you have uploaded all your CSVs you can simply visit the graph builder page and start creating your graphs.

Getting Started

Make sure you are logged in as the system needs to know who the uploaded files belong to.

Head over to the data loader and start uploading your CSVs.

Once you have uploaded your CSVs and you're happy to proceed, you can move onto the fun bit. Head over to graph builder and start building your graph!
